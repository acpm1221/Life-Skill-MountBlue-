# Learning Process

## Question 1: What is the Feynman Technique?

The Feynman Technique is a simple way to learn something properly.  
You try to explain what you learned using very easy words, as if you are teaching a child.  
If you cannot explain it clearly, it means you need to study that part again.

## Question 3: What are focused and diffused modes of thinking?

### Focused Mode
This mode is used when we concentrate deeply on a task.  
It helps when solving problems we already understand.

### Diffused Mode
This mode happens when the mind is relaxed.  
It helps in understanding new topics and coming up with creative solutions.

## Question 4: What steps should you follow when learning something new?

- Break the topic into small parts  
- Learn just enough to find and fix mistakes  
- Remove distractions like phone or TV  
- Practice regularly for at least 20 hours  

---

## Question 5: What actions will you take to improve your learning?

- Explain new topics in my own words  
- Study in short, focused sessions with breaks  
- Take short breaks when stuck to think better
