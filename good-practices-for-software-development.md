# Good Practices for Software Development – Review

## Question 1: Which point were new to you?

- I learned that **group chats** are better than private messages because they keep everyone informed.
- I now know the right way to **ask questions**: explain the problem, mention what I tried, and share screenshots or error logs.
- **Turning on video** during meetings helps build better team relationships.

## Question 2: Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas for making progress in that area?

I need to improve my **communication** and **focus**.

- I will **ask for help earlier** and explain my problems clearly. I will also **update the team** if a task takes more time.
- To improve focus, I will **keep my phone on silent**, avoid social media during work, and work in **focused blocks with short breaks**.
